    What happens before nine

      Since 2014

      One oven, one starter, no shortcuts

      We opened with a single deck oven and a starter carried from a bakery in Pondicherry. Both are still here. The starter gets fed twice a day, and it has never been replaced — the sourdough you buy this morning is a direct descendant of the first loaf we sold.

      We bake to a number, not to demand. When the croissants are gone at half nine, they're gone. It costs us sales and it's the only way the quality holds.
