Why us

What we do differently

Title checked first

Every listing goes through an independent title search before we photograph it. If the paperwork doesn't hold up, it never reaches this page.

We survey in person

An advisor walks each property and measures it. Carpet area on our listings is the measured figure, not the builder's brochure number.

Transparent pricing

Per-square-foot history for the street is shared before you make an offer. You see the comparable sales we're basing our advice on.

One advisor throughout

The person who shows you the property is the person who handles the paperwork. No handovers between departments mid-transaction.
